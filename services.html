Professional Services & Academic Consulting

I provide selective academic and professional consulting services in materials science, nuclear engineering, and computational modeling. These services are intended for researchers, graduate students, early-career faculty, startups, and industry professionals.

Academic & Research Services

  • PhD & Graduate Research Review – proposal structuring, thesis feedback, and research direction.
  • Manuscript Writing & Review – journal-ready editing, technical depth enhancement.
  • Grant & Proposal Development – SBIR/STTR, DOE, NSF, DoD technical narratives.
  • Peer Review & Technical Evaluation – independent assessment of manuscripts and proposals.

Professional & Immigration-Related Consulting

  • EB-1A (Extraordinary Ability) – academic profile evaluation and evidence strategy.
  • NIW / EB-2 – national importance framing and research positioning.

Disclaimer

Services are provided strictly for academic and professional guidance. No legal advice is offered. Immigration consulting is limited to research profile evaluation and documentation strategy.
