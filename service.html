Professional Services & Academic Consulting

I provide selective academic and professional consulting services in the areas of materials science, nuclear engineering, and research development. These services are intended for students, researchers, early-career faculty, startups, and industry professionals.

Academic & Research Services

  • PhD & Graduate Research Review
    Proposal structuring, thesis review, research direction, and technical feedback.
  • Manuscript Writing & Review
    Journal-ready manuscript editing, technical strengthening, reviewer-response preparation.
  • Grant & Proposal Writing Support
    SBIR/STTR, DOE, NSF, DoD concept development, technical narrative, and feasibility sections.
  • Peer Review & Evaluation
    Independent review of research proposals, manuscripts, and technical reports.

Professional & Immigration Consulting

  • EB-1A (Extraordinary Ability) Consultation
    Academic profile evaluation, evidence strategy, and expert letter guidance.
  • NIW / EB-2 Consultation
    Research positioning, national importance framing, and supporting documentation strategy.

Disclaimer

Services are provided strictly for academic and professional guidance. I do not provide legal advice. Immigration-related consulting is limited to research profile evaluation and documentation strategy only.
