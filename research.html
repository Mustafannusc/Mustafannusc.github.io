Research Interests

Core Research Areas

  • Material performance modeling under extreme environments
  • Atomic-scale and multiscale simulation of structural materials
  • Thermo-mechanical behavior of materials at high temperature
  • Dislocation, grain boundary, and defect interactions
  • Radiation damage and defect evolution in nuclear materials

Advanced Materials Systems

  • Oxide Dispersion Strengthened (ODS) steels
  • High-Entropy Alloys (HEAs)
  • Refractory Multi-Principal Element Alloys (RMPEAs)
  • Multilayered Metallic Composites (MMLCs)
  • 2D materials for structural and functional applications
  • Molten salts for advanced nuclear and energy systems

Computational & Data-Driven Approaches

  • Molecular dynamics (classical and ML-driven)
  • Density functional theory (DFT-based insights)
  • Machine learning for composition–structure–property relationships
  • Physics-informed, data-driven materials modeling
  • Integration of ICME workflows with experimental validation

Application Domains

  • Advanced nuclear fission and fusion systems
  • Aerospace and hypersonic materials
  • Radiation-tolerant structural materials
  • Additive manufacturing of high-temperature alloys
