Short Bio

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher and Adjunct Teaching Professor in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), USA, I am the Co-Founder of Material Innovation Solutions, a startup focused on computationally driven design and optimization of advanced materials for extreme environments. I received my Ph.D. in Nuclear Engineering and Technology. Prior to joining WPI, I held postdoctoral and visiting research appointments at Missouri University of Science and Technology, The University of Alabama, and Xi‚Äôan Jiaotong University. My research interests center on atomistic and multiscale modeling of high-temperature structural materials, including oxide-dispersion-strengthened (ODS) steels, high-entropy alloys, and refractory alloys, with applications in nuclear energy, aerospace systems, and advanced manufacturing.
