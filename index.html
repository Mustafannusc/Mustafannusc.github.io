Short Bio

I am a computational materials scientist working on atomistic and multiscale modeling of high-temperature structural materials, including oxide dispersion strengthened steels, high-entropy alloys, and refractory alloys for nuclear and aerospace applications. My research integrates molecular dynamics, machine-learning interatomic potentials, and ICME frameworks.
